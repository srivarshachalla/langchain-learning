BIGSTEP TECHNOLOGIES — HR POLICY DOCUMENT

LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to 24 paid leaves per year.
Leaves are credited on the 1st of January every year.
Unused leaves can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.
Employees must apply for leave at least 3 days in advance.
Emergency leaves require manager approval within 24 hours.
Maternity leave is 26 weeks as per government regulations.
Paternity leave is 5 working days.

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week.
WFH requires prior approval from the reporting manager.
WFH is not permitted on Mondays and Fridays.
Employees must be reachable on Slack and email during WFH.
WFH can be revoked if performance is impacted.

OFFICE TIMING POLICY
Standard office hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM Monday to Friday.
Employees must log a minimum of 8 hours per working day.
Late arrivals beyond 10:00 AM require prior intimation.
Early departure requires manager approval.
Attendance is tracked via the biometric system.

APPRAISAL POLICY
Performance appraisals are conducted twice a year.
Appraisal cycles run in April and October.
Ratings are given on a scale of 1 to 5.
Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for promotion.
Salary increments are based on appraisal rating and company performance.

REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
Travel reimbursements must be submitted within 7 days of travel.
Maximum cab reimbursement is Rs 500 per day.
Meal reimbursements are allowed up to Rs 200 per day during client visits.
All reimbursements require original bills and manager approval.

RESIGNATION POLICY
Employees must serve a notice period of 60 days.
Notice period can be reduced with mutual agreement.
Full and final settlement is processed within 45 days of last working day.
Employees must return all company assets before the last working day.
